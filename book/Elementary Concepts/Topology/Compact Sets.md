# Compact Sets

````{prf:theorem}
:label: thm:9

Closed subsets of a compact set are compact.

````

````{prf:theorem}
:label: thm:10

Let $\left\{K_\alpha\right\}_{\alpha \in I}$ be a family of compact subsets in topological space $X$ where $I$ is an arbitrary index set. If for any **finite** subset $J \subset I$, we have $\bigcap_{\alpha \in J} K_\alpha\neq\emptyset$, then $\bigcap_{\alpha \in I} K_\alpha\neq\emptyset$.

````
